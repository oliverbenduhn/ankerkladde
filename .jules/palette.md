## 2024-04-18 - Missing ARIA Labels for form inputs\n**Learning:** Form inputs like input type="text" and textarea in many places (e.g. barcode.php, admin.php, index.php) do not have corresponding <label> tags and rely entirely on `placeholder` attributes or implicit context, which causes accessibility issues. According to the a11y convention, they must use an `aria-label` attribute to describe their purpose rather than relying exclusively on placeholder attributes.\n**Action:** Add `aria-label` to form inputs and textareas that lack visible labels.
